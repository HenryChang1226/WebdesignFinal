Bariloche

DISCOVER

San Carlos de Bariloche is one of the most important cities in Patagonia and the largest.

It is located within the Nahuel Huapi National Park and on the shores of Lake Nahuel Huapi. At the base of the Andes, the city is a world-famous ski destination set in a landscape that offers all of Argentina's natural wonders.

Visitors can experience snow, lakes and quiet beaches, along with vibrant nightclubs and gourmet cuisine.
